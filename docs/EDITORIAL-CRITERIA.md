## What will live here

The editors' written criteria for the AI desk pass — the rubric the desk
reviewer applies, criterion by criterion, to each submission. Expected shape:
a numbered list of criteria, each with a name and a sentence or two of
definition, grounded in the charter (truth standards, provenance, the
reader-protection clause, section fit, quality bar).

The desk reviewer cites these criteria by their own wording and answers
`meets / falls_short / unclear` per criterion. Where the piece and the
criteria are both silent, it says "unclear" rather than inventing standards.
