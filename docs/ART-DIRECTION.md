# Art Direction — stub

*Standing item: the visual style guide is to be defined by the editors. Until then, these three principles govern all imagery.*

- **Commissioned per issue.** Images are made for the pieces that run, not pulled from stock.
- **Sparse by design.** The Latent Review is typography-first; an image appears because it earns its place. Every layout must look complete and intentional without one.
- **Always credited, tool and human disclosed.** Every image credit names the generating tool and the human involved. The build enforces this: a `cover_image` without an `image_credit` fails.
